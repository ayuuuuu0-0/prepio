# PRODUCT.md

# What Is Prepio?

Prepio is not an interview preparation platform.

Prepio is a progression game where becoming interview-ready is the reward.

Users do not wake up wanting to solve questions.

Users wake up wanting:

* Better jobs
* Better companies
* Better salaries
* Career growth

Prepio transforms interview preparation into a visible journey.

The goal is to make users feel:

"I am closer to my dream company today than I was yesterday."

---

# Product Philosophy

Duolingo sells progress.

Pokemon sells collection.

Clash Royale sells advancement.

Prepio sells career progression.

Every feature must reinforce progress.

If a feature does not improve:

* Progress
* Motivation
* Retention
* Competition
* Achievement

It should not exist.

# Product Pillars

## 1. Journey

The journey is the product.

Questions are content.

Journey is progression.

Structure:

World

↓

Node

↓

Skill

↓

Question Pool

↓

Question

Users progress through worlds.

Not question lists.

---

## 2. Readiness

Readiness is the primary metric.

Not XP.

Not question count.

Not streaks.

Users should always know:

How interview-ready they are.

Example:

Google Readiness 74%

Amazon Readiness 68%

Meta Readiness 59%

Every readiness score must be explainable.

---

## 3. Companions

Companions are emotional progression systems.

Not profile pictures.

Companions:

* React
* Evolve
* Celebrate
* Encourage

Companions create attachment.

---

## 4. Skills

Questions are not the foundation.

Skills are.

Example:

Arrays

├── Two Pointers

├── Sliding Window

├── Prefix Sum

└── Hash Maps

The platform must understand skills before it can understand progress.

---

## 5. Worlds

Worlds create destinations.

Examples:

Foundation Forest

Problem Solving Canyon

System Design City

Google Mountain

Amazon Jungle

Meta City

Worlds should feel memorable.

Not educational categories.

---

## 6. Collection

Humans love collecting.

Users collect:

Achievements

Badges

Titles

Companion Evolutions

Skins

World Trophies

Collection creates retention.

---

# Design Philosophy

Prepio must never look like:

* Government software
* Enterprise SaaS
* Admin dashboard
* Analytics platform

Prepio should feel like:

Duolingo

Pokemon

Clash Royale

Modern gaming products

---

# Visual Rules

Every screen must contain:

* Progress
* Color
* Motion
* Companion
* Reward

Every action should produce feedback.

No silent progression.

---

# Dashboard Philosophy

Dashboard is the most important screen.

Users should immediately understand:

* Where they are
* How they are progressing
* What they should do next

The dashboard is not analytics.

The dashboard is motivation.

---

# What We Are Not

We are not:

* LeetCode
* GeeksForGeeks
* InterviewBit
* Question banks

Questions are a means.

Progression is the product.
